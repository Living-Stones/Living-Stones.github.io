Jan 27 | Palestine and Theology

This talk will introduce us to Palestinian Christians and Palestinian Theology. We will earn more about how Christians in the Holy Land relate to their identity, the conflict and God. In light of the devastation, death and suffering in Gaza and other places in the Palestine, how do Christians respond in this the Holy land?
